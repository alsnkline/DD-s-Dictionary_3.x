The Dyslexic's Dictionary

I have had to work hard to manage my reading and writing challenges all my life and am now supporting my daughter as she starts the same journey. I decided to create DD's dictionary (The Dyslexic’s Dictionary) to give those having difficulty spelling, a tool for independent discovery of an unknown spelling or a quick check of an uncertain one.

Thank you to everyone who have clicked on the 'Talk to us' option and provided fantastic ideas and encouragement. I have been able to include a number of suggestions and plan to add more.

Future enhancements

Please keep your thougths and ideas for future enhancements flowing in. Here is an updated list of ideas:

  • The ability to immediately add your own words
  • Additional word grouping, eg hard to spell words ideal for adult Dyslexic’s
  • Sounds like search
  • Easy exploration of words that have UK/US differences
  • Present related words together eg following, follows, followed, follow.
  • Show easily confused word groups eg for, of, from, form

Thank you - Alison
